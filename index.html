Chapter 3 — the pacing slows down intentionally. Inoue is making you sit with Musashi instead of rushing you forward.

Kojiro feels peaceful in a way Musashi does not. His silence makes strength feel less like domination and more like presence.

Volume 10 — the fight choreography barely needs words. The page rhythm carries the emotion by itself.

The story keeps asking whether becoming stronger is worth it if you lose the ability to be gentle.

Vagabond made me realise I had been confusing movement with progress.

The contrast between Musashi and Kojiro makes the idea of strength feel much less simple.
